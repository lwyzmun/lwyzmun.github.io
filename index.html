What is Model United Nations(MUN)?

Model United Nations is a student conference with the aim of bringing the negotiations and experience of a real UN conference to life. Academically we not only aim to simulate the United Nations and grant our delegates the opportunity to expand their global views and leadership talents but also cultivate a new generation of young innovators ready to become the present of the future.

on Wikipedia

What is
LWYZMUN?

LWYZMUN was founded by Bright-Moon Jiao who is a handsome but unmarried English teacher. He likes doing someting different from the other teachers in Laiwu No.1 Middle School, which is also meant to symbolize a outstanding talent in Mr.Jiao's body. First and second grade students gather here to discuss, debate, and to solve a variety of current global and local issues. LWYZMUN can expand the learning horizons of every MUNer,of course,it should include you!
